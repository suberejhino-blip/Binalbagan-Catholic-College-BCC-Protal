About BCC

BCC is committed to academic excellence, leadership, and character formation.

We help students become globally competitive and career-ready professionals.

BCC Vision Christ-centered advocates of excellence, social justice. stewardship, and societal transformation in a rapidly changing world.

BCC Mission A Catholic educational institution run by the Presentation Sisters stewardship, of the Blessed Virgin Mary in the Philippines. inspired by the mission of Christ and the charism of Venerable Nano Nagle that upholds Catholic ideals, and provides quality, holistic, and transformative education to 21st-century learners delivered through relevant instruction, research, and community outreach by competent and committed educators who work in synergy for the sustainable advancement of humanity and our common home
